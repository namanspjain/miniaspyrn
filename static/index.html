About Us

Welcome to Aspyrn, this is the space for everyone to experiment with and explore the practice of Yoga
with Artificial Intelligence.

Aspyrn is founded on belief that Yoga should be accessible to all.
We firmly believe that when technology and ancient methodologies integrate then the results are wonderful.

Aspyrn offers you to learn and practice poses at your own pace and with the help of Artificial Intelligence.
